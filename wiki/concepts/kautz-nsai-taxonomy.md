Henry Kautz's six-type taxonomy of neurosymbolic computing systems, introduced in the 2020 AAAI Robert S. Engelmore Memorial Lecture ("The Third AI Summer"): Type 1 (Symbolic Neuro Symbolic), Type 2 (Symbolic[Neuro]), Type 3 (Neuro;Symbolic), Type 4 (Neuro:Symbolic→Neuro), Type 5 (NeuroSymbolic), Type 6 (Neuro[Symbolic]).

Classification note: assigned as concept (a named classification scheme with a definition), but also_type framework because the taxonomy organizes an entire research paradigm.
