A mode of AI participation in which the system functions as a constructive challenger rather than an executor or assistant — surfacing counterarguments, alternative viewpoints, and critical questions to mitigate human cognitive biases and reduce the likelihood of catastrophic rare-event errors in high-risk, high-complexity decision-making contexts where human judgment retains primary authority.
