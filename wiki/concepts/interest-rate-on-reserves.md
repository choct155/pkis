The rate paid by the Federal Reserve on commercial bank reserves held as deposits at the Fed; introduced to induce banks to hold the massive excess reserves created by QE programs, it equals the FF target rate and, under QE, transforms reserves from unique high-powered money (with opportunity cost) into ordinary short-term debt instruments.
