Daniel Kahneman's dual-process cognitive model: System 1 is fast, automatic, pattern-recognition-based processing that converts raw sensory data to symbols (analogized in AI to neural networks); System 2 is slow, deliberative, knowledge-driven reasoning supporting abstraction, analogy, and planning (analogized to symbolic AI); the neurosymbolic paradigm draws explicitly on this distinction to motivate combining neural and symbolic components.
