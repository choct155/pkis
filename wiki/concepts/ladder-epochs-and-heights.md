## Definition
For a random walk {S_n}, the first strict ascending ladder epoch N = inf{n >= 1 : S_n > 0} is the first time the walk sets a new record high; its iterates N(k) are the times of successive records and the values S_{N(k)} are the ascending ladder heights. The descending pair (Nbar = inf{n >= 1 : S_n <= 0}, S_{Nbar(k)}) is defined symmetrically. Both {N(k)} and {S_{N(k)}} are renewal processes (possibly terminating).
