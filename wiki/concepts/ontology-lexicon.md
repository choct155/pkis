An ontology lexicon is a declarative, theory-neutral specification of how the vocabulary of a domain ontology (entities, classes, relations) is verbalized in a particular natural language; the *lemon* (Lexicon Model for Ontologies) is the primary RDF-based standard for representing such lexica.
