Asymptotic distributions arising when parameters of one distribution approach limiting values, providing useful approximations: e.g., Binomial(n,p) → Poisson(np) as n→∞, p→0; Binomial(n,p) → Normal via the Central Limit Theorem for large n; gamma → normal; geometric → exponential — enabling analytic approximations and simulation shortcuts.
