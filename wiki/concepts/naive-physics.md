Naive Physics (Hayes, 1978–79) is the research program of formally representing everyday physical knowledge — the commonsense understanding of liquids, containers, motion, space, and causality that humans apply effortlessly but that is difficult to encode in AI systems; QP theory (Forbus) is a contribution to naive physics that formalizes physical processes as the ontological primitives of commonsense physical reasoning.
