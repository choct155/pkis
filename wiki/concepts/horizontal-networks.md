Putnam's concept of lateral associational ties between social equals — as opposed to vertical bonds of authority — that build the mutual familiarity and reciprocity norms underpinning generalized trust in a society.

Classification note: assigned as concept but may be principle because horizontal networking functions as a normative prescription for trust-building as much as a descriptive category.
