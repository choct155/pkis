The capacity of an AI system to monitor and regulate its own reasoning processes — including detecting inconsistencies, recognizing when additional reasoning is needed, and updating beliefs in response to detected errors — currently lacking in LLMs, which exhibit metacognitive myopia (evaluating source validity poorly), systematic overconfidence, and goal misunderstanding in open-ended settings.

Classification note: assigned as concept because metacognition names a specific cognitive capacity with a precise definition and measurable boundary; also typed as problem because current LLMs' deficiency in metacognition is a primary motivating challenge for agentic AI development.
