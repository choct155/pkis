Group theory is the branch of abstract algebra studying sets equipped with a binary operation satisfying closure, associativity, identity, and invertibility, together with the structure-preserving maps (homomorphisms) between them.

## Reading Path
- [[carrell-groups-matrices-vectors-ch02]] (unread) — primary treatment: groups, subgroups, cosets, Lagrange's theorem, normal subgroups, quotient groups, First Isomorphism Theorem
- [[carrell-groups-matrices-vectors-ch11]] (unread) — advanced theorems: Sylow theorems, orbit-stabilizer, structure of finite abelian groups, solvable groups
