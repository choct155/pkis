Qualitative reasoning is a symbolic AI paradigm that represents and reasons about physical and causal systems using discrete, order-of-magnitude abstractions (sign, direction, landmark values) rather than precise numerical simulation — enabling predictions about system behavior (which states are reachable, whether quantities increase or decrease, whether stable equilibria exist) from incomplete or imprecise knowledge.
