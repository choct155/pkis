The arena in which alternative management teams compete for the rights to manage corporate resources, with shareholders accepting or rejecting competing takeover offers; hostile takeovers serve as a discipline-of-last-resort mechanism when internal governance (board, managerial labor market) fails, and successful tender offers generate approximately 30% abnormal returns for target shareholders.
