## Definition
The subfield concerned with encoding knowledge about the world in a form a system can reason over — concepts, relations, constraints, and the axioms that govern them. The foundational discipline the intensional-grounding cluster draws on to design experiments: intensional class definitions and necessary/sufficient membership conditions are KR constructs.
