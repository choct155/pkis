Asymmetric neurosymbolic coupling is a design pattern in which symbolic ontological knowledge constrains one direction of an agentic AI pipeline more strongly than another — specifically, where ontologies constrain agent inputs (context assembly, tool discovery, governance thresholds) while output-side constraints (response validation, compliance checking) require additional mechanisms not yet fully realized; introduced in Luong (2026) for enterprise LLM systems.
