The multi-dimensional structure of systematic risk premiums beyond CAPM's single market beta, including value, momentum, carry, liquidity, and volatility premiums, which represent compensation for distinct sources of systematic risk that active investment strategies may legitimately access and package for investors.

Classification note: assigned as concept but may be framework because the multi-factor view of risk premiums organizes the entire modern empirical asset pricing research program.

## Reading Path
- [[cochrane-finance-function-2013]] (unread) — primary treatment; argues that conventional alpha-beta analysis misses this rich structure and that sophisticated institutional investors may rationally pay for exposure to alternative risk factors
