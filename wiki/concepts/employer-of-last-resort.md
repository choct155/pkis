A government policy proposal in which the state acts as employer of last resort, guaranteeing a fixed-wage job to any willing worker; functions as a buffer-stock mechanism that automatically stabilizes employment and anchors the price level by setting the floor wage for the private sector.

Classification note: assigned as concept but may also be framework because it organizes a full set of fiscal, monetary, and labor-market policies around a single operational mechanism.
