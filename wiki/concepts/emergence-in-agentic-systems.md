The phenomenon by which an agentic system composed of comparatively simple agents exhibits advanced capabilities — including enhanced cognition, causal reasoning, and metacognitive awareness — that arise from interaction dynamics at the agent-environment, agent-agent, and human-agent interfaces rather than from the individual capabilities of component agents.
