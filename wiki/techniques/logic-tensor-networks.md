Logic Tensor Networks (LTN) are a neurosymbolic framework that implements "Real Logic" — a many-valued, end-to-end differentiable first-order logic — by grounding FOL symbols (constants, predicates, functions) as real-valued tensors; logical formulas become differentiable loss functions that maximize satisfiability, enabling joint learning and reasoning via gradient descent.
