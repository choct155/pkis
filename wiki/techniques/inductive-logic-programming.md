Inductive Logic Programming (ILP) is a machine learning technique that learns logic programs (Horn clause rules) automatically from positive and negative examples and background knowledge; approaches are bottom-up (generalizing from specific ground clauses) or top-down (refining general rules toward specific instances), with differentiable ILP variants enabling gradient-based rule learning from noisy data.
