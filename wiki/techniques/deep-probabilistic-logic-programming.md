Deep Probabilistic Logic Programming (exemplified by DeepProbLog, Manhaeve et al. 2018) extends probabilistic logic programs (ProbLog) by allowing neural predicates — facts whose truth probability is computed by a neural network rather than specified manually — enabling end-to-end learning of programs that combine neural perception with probabilistic symbolic reasoning; uses a probabilistic (not fuzzy) interpretation of logical variables.
