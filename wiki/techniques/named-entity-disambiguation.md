## Definition
The task of mapping a surface mention of an entity to its correct referent in a knowledge base, resolving ambiguity across surface variation (different strings, same entity) and surface collision (same string, different entities). NED/NER accuracy is the primary task instrumented by the Intensional Grounding cluster: the hypothesis is that ontological type constraints and token-to-ontological-class distance improve resolution beyond what distributional statistics alone achieve.
