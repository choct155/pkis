A solution method for DSGE models that obtains polynomial approximations to decision rules and value functions by taking successive derivatives of the equilibrium conditions (or value function) with respect to states and the perturbation parameter — the standard deviation of exogenous shocks — and applying an implicit function theorem; a third-order approximation is the minimum needed for time-varying risk premia and to identify risk aversion, since the first-order solution is certainty-equivalent and risk aversion first enters at second order.
