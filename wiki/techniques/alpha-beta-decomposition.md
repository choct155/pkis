The technique of regressing a fund's excess returns on a benchmark index's excess returns to decompose performance into beta (systematic risk exposure, replicable without fees) and alpha (excess return beyond benchmark, interpreted as manager skill); Cochrane (2013) argues this approach is inadequate because conventional CAPM beta misses the rich multi-factor structure of risk premiums that active managers may legitimately access.
