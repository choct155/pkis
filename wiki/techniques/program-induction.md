Program induction is the task of learning programs (logic rules, functional programs, or structured hypotheses) from input-output examples; differential program induction (Evans & Grefenstette 2018) upgrades classical ILP to learn explanatory logic rules from noisy data using neural predicates and gradient-based optimization, bridging symbolic program synthesis with deep learning.
