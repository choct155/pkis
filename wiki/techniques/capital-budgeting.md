The process of evaluating potential investment projects by comparing their expected return to the firm's cost of capital; MM establish that investment should be guided solely by whether a project's yield exceeds the cost of capital for its risk class, independently of how the project is financed; CAPM provides the risk-adjusted discount rate via β.
