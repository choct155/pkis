Three-stage interactive explainer for confusion-matrix-based accuracy estimation.
Stage 1: live confusion matrix with sliders for true prevalence, sensitivity, specificity.
Stage 2: prevalence estimation via confusion matrix inversion with algebraic derivation
shown live. Stage 3: Bayesian updating — add gold observations one at a time and watch
Beta posterior distributions sharpen; includes precision vs N_gold chart and information
gained tracker. Covers the dark error rate concept and the revision-process passive
accuracy signal.
