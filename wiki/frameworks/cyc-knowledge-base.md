Cyc (Cycorp) is a large-scale AI knowledge base system founded by Doug Lenat (1984–2023) that encodes millions of commonsense rules of thumb and domain facts in CycL — a higher-order predicate logic — together with an inference engine that reasons in real time via heuristic theorem proving; microtheories (context partitions) enable tractable reasoning by localizing inference to relevant sub-domains, partially overcoming the expressiveness-tractability tradeoff that forces simpler symbolic systems to use less expressive logics like knowledge graphs.
