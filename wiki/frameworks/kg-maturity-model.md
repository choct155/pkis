A staged model of organizational knowledge graph adoption adapted from the Ontology Maturing Process Model, progressing through three phases: (1) Folksonomy — informal tag-based annotation; (2) Formalization — organizing tags into SKOS controlled vocabularies and taxonomies with hierarchical and similarity relations; (3) Specification — developing selected concepts into full OWL/RDFS class structures with properties, axioms, and constraints enabling reasoning and inferencing.

Classification note: assigned as framework because it organizes concepts (maturity phases), principles (progressive formalization), and techniques (folksonomy tagging, SKOS modeling, OWL specification) into a coherent developmental arc for KG cultivation; but also_type concept because it can be studied as "what is this model?" — a defined maturity instrument with assessment criteria.
