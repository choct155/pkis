A systematic framework for assigning AI roles based on task characteristics rather than technological capabilities: tasks are classified by risk level (low/intermediate/high) and complexity (low/moderate/high), yielding a 3×3 matrix that maps to three AI roles — Autonomous (low-risk, low-complexity), Assistive/Collaborative (intermediate), and Adversarial (high-risk, high-complexity) — with stakeholder preferences as a third dimension that can override efficiency-based role assignments.
