Statistical Relational Learning (SRL) is a research framework that unifies symbolic logic and probabilistic/statistical inference by extending graphical models to relational domains — controlling expressiveness through finite-domain relational extensions to Bayesian networks (Koller & Pfeffer, Getoor et al.) and Markov Logic Networks (Richardson & Domingos); SRL is the foundational lineage from which modern probabilistic neurosymbolic AI (DeepProbLog, ProbLog, Markov logic) descends.
