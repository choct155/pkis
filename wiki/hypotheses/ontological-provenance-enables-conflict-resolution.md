## Formal Statement
Knowing which source contributed which component and the ontological relationship between components enables more accurate cross-source conflict detection and resolution than heuristic credibility weighting.

## Motivation
Composite responses draw from sources of varying reliability; ontological provenance turns aggregation from a heuristic into a principled inference.
