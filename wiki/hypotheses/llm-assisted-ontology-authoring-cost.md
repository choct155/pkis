## Formal Statement
Using LLMs as ontology authoring assistants (proposing definitions, properties, class hierarchies) with human review as the gate reduces marginal maintenance cost relative to human authorship, while preserving consistency and referential integrity.

## Motivation
Ontology maintenance cost is the main objection to ontological scaffolding; if LLMs cut it without sacrificing integrity, the economics change.
