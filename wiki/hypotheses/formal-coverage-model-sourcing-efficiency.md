## Formal Statement
Organizations maintaining a formal ontological coverage model exhibit lower redundant source acquisition and higher marginal coverage yield per new source than opinion-driven sourcing.

## Motivation
This is the meta-hypothesis behind hypothesis-driven sourcing: an explicit coverage model lets gaps be identified before acquisition rather than discovered after.
