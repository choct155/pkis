Develops sharp and fuzzy regression discontinuity designs: the running variable, threshold assignment, local linear regression near the cutoff, bandwidth selection, continuity assumption, the McCrary density test for manipulation, and the LATE interpretation for fuzzy RDD — using Angrist and Lavy (1999) Maimonides' Rule and Card et al. (2008) Medicare eligibility as canonical applications.
