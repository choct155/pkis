## Notes
Canonical reference for modular ontology modeling. Introduces ontology design patterns (ODPs) as reusable mini-ontologies with defined scope and articulation points. Inseparability criterion: a module M is self-contained relative to signature Sigma if M is Sigma-inseparable from the full ontology — a testable formal self-containment condition directly applicable to PKIS concept nodes. Class granularity as measurable diagnostic for over/under-specification.
