Develops the difference-in-differences estimator: the 2×2 DiD setup, the parallel trends assumption, two-way fixed effects implementation, pre-trends testing, and heterogeneous treatment timing — using Card and Krueger (1994) minimum wage study in New Jersey and Pennsylvania as the canonical application.
