Covers Bayesian inference: hierarchical Bayesian models, common conjugate Bayesian models (normal with unknown mean and variance, normal linear model, multinomial), Bayesian networks as graphical representations of conditional independence, posterior asymptotic normality (Bernstein-von Mises), prior specification and conjugacy, and Bayesian model comparison via Bayes factors and marginal likelihoods.
