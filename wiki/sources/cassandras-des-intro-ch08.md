Develops queueing theory as a major application domain for stochastic DES: A/B/m/K Kendall notation, Little's Law (L=λW), Markovian queues (M/M/1, M/M/m, M/M/1/K), open and closed queueing networks, product-form networks (Jackson networks), and non-Markovian queues via stages and mean value analysis for M/G/1 systems.
