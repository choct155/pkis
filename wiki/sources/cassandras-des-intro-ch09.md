Introduces Markov Decision Processes as the framework for optimal control of stochastic DES: cost criteria (discounted, average), the Bellman optimality equation, dynamic programming solution via value iteration and policy iteration, extensions to average-cost problems, and applications to queueing control (admission, routing, scheduling).
