Formal treatment of counterfactual reasoning within SCMs via the twin-network construction. Defines Y_x(u), shows the three-step procedure (abduction → action → prediction) for evaluating counterfactuals, and unifies the framework with Rubin's potential outcomes. Establishes counterfactuals as Level 3 of the Ladder of Causation.
