Covers the mechanics and statistical methodology of DES simulation: event-scheduling and process-oriented simulation schemes, random number generation (linear congruential), random variate generation (inverse transform, convolution, acceptance-rejection), and statistical output analysis for both terminating and non-terminating simulations including confidence intervals and warm-up detection.
