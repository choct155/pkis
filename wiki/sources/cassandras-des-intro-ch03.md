Presents the Ramadge-Wonham supervisory control framework: controlled DES with enabled/disabled events, the Controllability Theorem, nonblocking supervisory control, modular control, control under partial observation (Controllability-and-Observability Theorem), normality, and decentralized control with conjunctive/disjunctive/combined architectures and coobservability.
