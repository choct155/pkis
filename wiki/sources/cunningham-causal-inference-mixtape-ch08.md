Develops the instrumental variables strategy: the three conditions for a valid instrument (relevance, exogeneity/independence, exclusion restriction), the first- and second-stage mechanics of 2SLS, the LATE interpretation (Angrist, Imbens, Rubin 1996), and the complier/defier/never-taker/always-taker typology — using the Angrist and Krueger (1991) quarter-of-birth instrument and Angrist and Evans (1998) sibling-sex instrument as leading examples.
