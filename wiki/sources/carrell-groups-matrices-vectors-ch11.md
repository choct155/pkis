Develops the advanced theory of finite groups: group actions and the orbit-stabilizer theorem, Cauchy's theorem, conjugacy classes, p-groups, finite subgroups of SO(3,R), the Sylow theorems, structure of finite abelian groups, the Chinese Remainder Theorem, solvable and simple groups, the Jordan–Hölder theorem, and an application to breaking the Enigma cipher via permutation group theory.
