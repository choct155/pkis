Develops the synthetic control method (Abadie and Gardeazabal 2003; Abadie, Diamond, Hainmueller 2010): constructing a pre-treatment-weighted combination of donor pool units to serve as a counterfactual for a single treated unit, assessing fit quality, and validating via in-space and in-time placebo tests — using California's Proposition 99 tobacco tax as the primary application.
