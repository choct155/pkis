Develops Petri net formalism as an alternative to automata for modeling concurrent DES: markings, firing rules, Petri net languages, comparison with automata expressiveness, coverability tree analysis, linear-algebraic techniques (P-invariants, T-invariants), and supervisory control of Petri nets via state-based specifications.
