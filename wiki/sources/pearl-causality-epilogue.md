Transcript of Pearl's 1996 public lecture at UCLA providing an accessible introduction to the causal revolution. Covers the history of causation in science, the correlation vs. causation distinction, and Pearl's vision for a formal science of cause and effect — a non-technical entry point to the book's themes.
