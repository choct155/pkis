Covers identification via selection on observables: subclassification, exact matching, nearest-neighbor matching, and propensity score methods (Rosenbaum and Rubin 1983) — using the Lalonde (1986) job training dataset and Dehejia and Wahba (1999) replication to illustrate covariate balance, common support, and the overlap assumption required for matching to work.
