Covers the frequentist inference toolkit: method of moments and least-squares estimation, confidence intervals (for means, variances, proportions, linear models), hypothesis testing (null/alternative hypotheses, p-values, power, ANOVA for normal linear models), cross-validation (PRESS statistic), and sufficiency with exponential families.
