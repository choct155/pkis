While RAG addresses critical limitations of parametric knowledge storage-such as factual inconsistency and domain inflexibility-it introduces new challenges in retrieval quality, grounding fidelity, pipeline efficiency, and robustness against noisy or adversarial inputs. ## Notes
Comprehensive survey of RAG architectures, enhancements, and robustness frontiers. Synthesizes evaluation dimensions across ARES, RAGAS, and related frameworks. Covers three core evaluation dimensions: context relevance, answer faithfulness, answer relevance — and their interdependence. Key finding: poor context relevance cascades into reduced faithfulness and answer relevance, underscoring the need for joint evaluation. Useful as a survey reference for the full RAG evaluation landscape and for establishing where our framework's structural dimensions (validity, structural coherence) are absent from the existing literature.
