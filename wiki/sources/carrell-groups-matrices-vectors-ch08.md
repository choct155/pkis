Develops eigentheory: the characteristic polynomial, diagonalizability criteria, eigenspace decomposition, the Cayley–Hamilton theorem, nilpotent matrices, the minimal polynomial, the Principal Axis Theorem (spectral theorem for self-adjoint mappings), and the rotation groups of R³ with applications to symmetry groups of the Platonic solids.
