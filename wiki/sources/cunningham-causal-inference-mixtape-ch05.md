Formalizes Rubin's potential outcomes framework: Y(0) and Y(1) as the two potential outcomes for each unit, SUTVA (no interference, single treatment version), the fundamental problem of causal inference (individual effects unobservable), and the decomposition of naive comparisons into ATE plus selection bias — illustrated with the Tennessee STAR class-size experiment (Krueger 1999).
