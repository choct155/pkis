Introduces timing into DES models: the clock structure formalism, timed automata with deterministic event-timing dynamics, timed Petri nets, the (max,+) dioid algebra for synchronization-dominated systems, timed automata with guards (Alur-Dill model with real-valued clocks and guard constraints), parallel composition of timed automata, and hybrid automata combining continuous dynamics with discrete events.
