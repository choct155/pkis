Introduces linear algebraic groups: GL(n,F) as an algebraic variety, reductive and semisimple groups, the classical groups, algebraic tori, the Weyl group, Borel subgroups, the conjugacy of Borel subgroups, flag varieties, the Bruhat decomposition of GL(n,F) and reductive groups, parabolic subgroups, linearly reductive groups, and Maschke's theorem on complete reducibility of representations.
