## Notes
HotpotQA: multi-hop reading comprehension over Wikipedia passages; reasoning across two documents. ~113,000 questions. Metric: EM and F1 on answer spans. Contamination risk: very high. Role: historically important benchmark that established multi-hop reasoning as a research area. Less directly relevant to VGT (text-based, not graph-based). Included for literature context — appears constantly in related work.
