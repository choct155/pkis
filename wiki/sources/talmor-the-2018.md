## Notes
ComplexWebQuestions (CWQ): hard KGQA benchmark extending WebQSP with constraints, conjunctions, superlatives. Up to 4-hop reasoning on Freebase. 59% require >2 hops. ~31,000 questions. Metric: Hits@1. Contamination risk: high. Role: primary hard benchmark for VGT evaluation. Where ToG's beam-search gains are most pronounced and the ELBO plateau convergence criterion shows most differentiation.
