Develops the Gibbs sampler from chained data augmentation, provides worked examples (rat growth, Poisson change-point, GLMs with random effects), and covers convergence assessment (Gibbs stopper, control variates). Introduces the Griddy Gibbs Sampler for non-standard conditionals and the Metropolis algorithm for direct posterior sampling without augmentation.
