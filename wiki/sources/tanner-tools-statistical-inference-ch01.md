Introduces the book's three taxonomies for organizing computational inference algorithms: by function (maximize, marginalize, simulate), by randomness (deterministic, non-iterative Monte Carlo, iterative Monte Carlo), and by augmentation strategy (augmentation vs. non-augmentation), providing a unified conceptual map for all subsequent chapters.
