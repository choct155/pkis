## Summary

Refines prediction-powered inference with a power-tuning coefficient lambda that optimally weights the prediction-based correction by the predictor's quality. PPI++ is computationally lighter and statistically never worse than classical (gold-only) inference, removing PPI's failure mode when predictions are weak. The production-ready variant of the PPI estimator.
