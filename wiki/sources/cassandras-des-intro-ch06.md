Extends timed automata to stochastic settings via stochastic clock structures; introduces the Generalized Semi-Markov Process (GSMP) as the canonical stochastic DES model; covers the Poisson counting process and its properties (memorylessness, superposition, residual lifetime paradox); shows that automata with Poisson clock structures are equivalent to continuous-time Markov chains.
