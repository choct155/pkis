Covers Schur triangularization, the normal matrix theorem, and the Principal Axis Theorem (short proof via Schur); then develops quadratic forms, their diagonalization, Hermitian forms, positive definite and positive semidefinite matrices; and concludes with Sylvester's Law of Inertia and the polar decomposition of a complex linear mapping.
