Covers state space models for time series: the unobserved components (UC) model with frequentist Kalman filter and Bayesian MCMC estimation, time-varying parameter models with Bayesian estimation via Gibbs sampling, and the stochastic volatility model with the auxiliary mixture sampling approach (Kim-Shephard-Chib) for tractable non-Gaussian inference.
