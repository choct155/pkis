## Notes
Overview of the first LLMs4OL challenge at ISWC 2024. Three tasks: term typing, taxonomy discovery, non-taxonomic relation extraction. Key finding: LLMs perform well on term typing and taxonomy but degrade sharply on boundary decisions — when to split a concept, when a relation implies node merging. Empirically confirms that concept boundary decisions are not automatable with current LLMs. Directly supports ontologist-as-boundary-arbitrator positioning in the continuous hardening mixture framework.
