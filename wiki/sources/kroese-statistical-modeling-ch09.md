A new chapter in the 2nd edition covering shrinkage estimators and regularization: the James-Stein estimator as motivation for shrinkage, ridge regression (L2 penalty, Gram matrix, bias-variance trade-off), lasso regression (L1 penalty, sparsity), and the Benjamini-Hochberg procedure for controlling the false discovery rate in multiple hypothesis testing.
