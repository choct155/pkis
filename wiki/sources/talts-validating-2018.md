## Summary
Verifying the correctness of Bayesian computation is challenging. This is especially true for complex models that are common in practice, as these require sophisticated model implementations and algorithms. In this paper we introduce \emph{simulation-based calibration} (SBC), a general procedure for validating inferences from Bayesian algorithms capable of generating posterior samples.
