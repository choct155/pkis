Introduces the *lemon* (Lexicon Model for Ontologies) — a theory-neutral, RDF-publishable declarative model for specifying how ontology entities, classes, and relations are verbalized in natural language; covers modeling of nouns, verbs, adjectives, and adverbs for different word classes.
