Develops the EM algorithm theory, its application in the exponential family, and five methods for standard errors after EM: direct numerical differentiation, the missing information principle, Louis' method, simulation-based, and using EM iterates. Introduces the Monte Carlo E-step (MCEM) for intractable E-steps.
