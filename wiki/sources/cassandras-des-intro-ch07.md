Provides a systematic treatment of discrete-time and continuous-time Markov chains: Chapman-Kolmogorov equations, state classification (transient, recurrent, ergodic), steady-state analysis via balance equations, irreducible and reducible chains, birth-death chains, uniformization technique for converting CTMCs to DTMCs, with queueing applications throughout.
