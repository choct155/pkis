Kressner and Ballani's chapter presents hierarchical matrices (H-matrices) in which off-diagonal blocks have low numerical rank due to smoothness of the underlying integral kernel, enabling O(n log² n) algorithms for matrix-vector products and approximate factorizations in PDE applications.
