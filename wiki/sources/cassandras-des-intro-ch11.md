Develops perturbation analysis as a gradient estimation framework for DES: infinitesimal perturbation analysis (IPA) for GI/G/1 queues and stochastic timed automata, conditions for IPA unbiasedness, smoothed perturbation analysis (SPA) for discontinuous sample functions, extensions to multiple customer classes, routing discontinuities, and blocking; concurrent estimation for rapid learning from a single sample path; IPA for stochastic hybrid automata and stochastic fluid models.
