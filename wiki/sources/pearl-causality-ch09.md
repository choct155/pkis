Defines and identifies three quantities: probability of necessity (PN), probability of sufficiency (PS), and probability of necessity and sufficiency (PNS). Formal grounding for legal/epidemiological questions like "was X a necessary cause of Y for this individual?" — connects counterfactual probability to observational data under monotonicity or exogeneity conditions.
