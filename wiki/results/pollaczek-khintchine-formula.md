## Definition
The Pollaczek-Khintchine formula gives the Laplace transform (equivalently the distribution) of the stationary waiting time W_infinity of an M/G/1 queue as a geometric mixture of convolutions of the integrated (equilibrium) service distribution: E e^{-lambda W_infinity} = (1 - rho) / (1 - rho * Bhat_0(lambda)), where rho < 1 is the traffic intensity and B_0 is the stationary-excess service law.
