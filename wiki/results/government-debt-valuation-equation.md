The equilibrium condition that the real value of nominal government liabilities (money plus debt) equals the present value of expected future primary surpluses: (M_t + B_t)/P_t = E_t[∑_{τ=0}^∞ (Λ_{t+τ}/Λ_t) × s_{t+τ}]; holds in virtually all models of money and inflation, and is the analytical centerpiece of the Fiscal Theory of the Price Level.
