MM Proposition II: the expected rate of return on equity of a levered firm equals the capitalization rate for an all-equity firm in its risk class plus a premium equal to the debt-to-equity ratio times the spread between the equity capitalization rate and the risk-free rate — i_j = ρ_k + (ρ_k − r)(D_j/S_j) — ensuring that the weighted average cost of capital remains constant regardless of leverage.
